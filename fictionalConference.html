description

Web Future Con is a one-day conference designed to ignite your passion for the ever-evolving world of web development. This immersive event brings together industry leaders, creative minds, and aspiring developers to explore the cutting-edge technologies and trends shaping the future of the web.

Key Speakers

Dr. sai:

A leading researcher and pioneer in the field of web technologies. Dr. Sai will be delivering the opening keynote address, titled "Web : joy of development" exploring the future trajectory of the web and its impact on various aspects of our lives.

Ms.Rani:

A renowned web development architect and entrepreneur. Ms. Rani will be discussing "Architeture life line of development process " focusing on the rise of Progressive Web Apps (PWAs) and their potential to revolutionize the user experience.

Mr. Ravi :

A prominent researcher and developer in the field of artificial intelligence (AI) for web applications. Mr. Ravi will be presenting a talk titled "Web with AI," exploring how AI can be leveraged to enhance web functionalities and create intelligent user experiences
